Dear Mom,

"You didn't even know..."

Mom, you didn't even know that the shopkeeper and I had accidentally forgotten completely about the medicine discount. When you scolded me, I tried to cover up my mistake by losing my temper, and I said some terrible things that I should never have muttered. You were completely right to be upset, and my reaction was entirely uncalled for.

I know how much hard work you put in for us every single day. My words were incredibly ungrateful and hurtful. I understand my mistake completely now. Please take all the time you need, but please know that I love you deeply and am truly sorry for my behavior.

My Promise: I will be much more responsible with tasks, keep my head cool, and never talk back to you like that again.

- Your Loving Child
